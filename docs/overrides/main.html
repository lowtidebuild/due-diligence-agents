Features

Everything the pipeline ships,
in a codebase your team owns.

No vendor lock-in. No per-seat pricing. Run it on your own infrastructure, with your own LLM provider.

🔗

Cross-domain analysis

A legal clause that threatens a finance projection. A tech dependency that undermines a commercial term. Compound risks surface automatically — named, cited, severity-ranked.

📌

Every finding, cited to source

Each conclusion traces back to a page number, section heading, and verbatim quote. Any claim can be verified in under 60 seconds. The pipeline halts rather than ship unverified output.

🔒

Your data never leaves your stack

Documents only travel as API calls to your configured LLM provider — Anthropic, AWS Bedrock, Google Vertex, or any gateway. Your infrastructure, your audit trail.

📊

Structured, auditable output

Interactive HTML report with Go/No-Go view, 16-sheet Excel export, JSON findings index, and IC memo template — structured for your advisors to verify and build on.
