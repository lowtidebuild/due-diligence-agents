Features

Built for the diligence,
not the demo.

No vendor lock-in. No per-seat pricing. Run inside your own infrastructure with your own LLM provider.

01

Cross-domain analysis

A legal clause that threatens a finance projection. A tech dependency that undermines a commercial term. Compound risks surface automatically — named, cited, and severity-ranked across all nine domains.

02

Every finding, cited to source

Each conclusion traces back to a page number, section heading, and verbatim quote. Verifiable in under 60 seconds. The pipeline halts rather than ship output it can't ground.

03

Your data stays in your stack

Documents travel only as API calls to your configured LLM provider — Anthropic, AWS Bedrock, Google Vertex, or any Anthropic-compatible gateway. Your infrastructure, your audit trail.

04

Structured, auditable output

Interactive HTML report with Go/No-Go view, 16-sheet Excel export, JSON findings index, and IC memo template — structured for your advisors to verify, annotate, and build on.
